ADVENT GIVING
The following opportunities are sponsored by the Mission Committee.

 

Angel & Mitten Trees:  Buy a gift for a child in Project Learn or donate mittens and scarves to a Bridgeport Elementary School student.

 

Alternate Giving Program:  This program gives you the opportunity to purchase a gift for someone on your Christmas list by making a donation to a local or international project that helps with people's needs in many ways.    For example, you can purchase a Bridgeport Rescue Mission meal, buy a household fixture for  Coastal Habitat for Humanity or purchase a farm animal for the International Heifer Project.  A complete list can be found at the Mission table in Brooks Hall each Sunday during Advent.  Gift cards will be available for your 'gift giving'.
